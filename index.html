Welcome to Preston Reservoir Little Athletics Centre Inc.

located at the Athletics Pavilion, Edwardes Lake Park in Reservoir.

 

Little Aths is a great sport for all kids aged 5-16, aligned with the Little Athletic values of

'FAMILY, FUN and FITNESS'

 

 

At PRLAC we focus on improvement and participation and love seeing kids involved in the great sport of Athletics! It's a great way to make new friends whilst learning new skills (some that will help other sports) and getting fit.

 

This summer season will run from October through until late March with a break over Christmas and New Years. The Cross Country season runs from April through to August.

 

Our weekly Competiton Days are usually held from 8:30am on Saturday mornings during summer.

FREE TRIAL

 

Not sure if Little Aths is for you? Just come on down and try it out for two weeks FOR FREE.

 

New athletes just need to click on the Trial Offer:

 

https://lavic.com.au/membership/new-member/trial-athlete-offer/

 

and select Option A to register for your free 2-week trial.

 

Remember to select ‘Preston/Reservoir LAC’ as your chosen centre.

If you would like to sponsor the club, please email prestonreservoir@lavic.com.au
