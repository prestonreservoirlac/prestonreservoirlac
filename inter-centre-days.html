INTER-CENTRE DAYS

PRLAC is involved in an Inter Centre competition with other Northern Metropolitan Centres comprising Hume, Craigieburn and Kilmore.

Hume hosts the 1st leg in November each season and the 2nd leg is shared around between the other three Centres

This is a great opportunity to introduce our athletes and parents to a wider competition outside of the traditional Region and State event.

Intercentre Days this 2021/22 Track and Field Season:

Intercentre Day 1- Saturday 27th November 2021 at Hume LAC

Intercentre Day 2- Saturday 19th February 2021 at Kilmore LAC
