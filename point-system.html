POINTS SYSTEM

Points are accumulated all season and totalled at the end of the year. At the end of the season, in each age group, boys and girls are separate, trophies are awarded to 1st, 2nd and 3rd and then the rest of the age group are awarded participation trophies.

 

Points are allocated event by event as per below:

1st place    12 pts

2nd place     10 pts

3rd place      8 pts

4th place      6 pts

5th place      4 pts

6th place       2 pts

 

Every place after this is then allocated 1 point. Where there is an equal place such as 1st and 2nd, 11 points are allocated each.
