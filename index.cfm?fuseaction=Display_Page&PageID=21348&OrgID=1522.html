COMMITEE MEMBERS REQUIRED

Have you have ever wanted to be a bigger part of the club than just an occassional parent helper, ever wanted to help make decisions on the day to day running or wanted to make a difference to your club?

Well now's your chance! We have a number of Committee positions up for grabs (well all of them actually).

So if you fancy yourself the next President, Treasurer or even just interested in becoming a general committee member email us at prestonreservoir@lavic.com.au and we'll send you a nomination form.

A club can only operate with a strong committee so we really need your help.
